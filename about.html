About Loot

Built at McMaster.
Built for students.

We saw the same problems every semester — overpriced textbooks, chaotic group chats, and zero trust. So we built the marketplace we wish existed.

"Canada has over a million university students and no verified campus marketplace. That's not a gap — that's the whole problem."

The problem

Every September, students at every Ontario university go through the same ritual. They post in scattered group chats, get lowballed by people with no connection to campus, and end up overpaying at the bookstore because nothing is organized or trustworthy.

Nothing has been built properly for Canada — no verification layer, no campus-first design, no way to know who's actually a student. That's the gap Loot is filling.

Why verification matters

General classifieds weren't built for campus life. When you're meeting someone in your residence lobby or outside the library, you want to know they're a student — not a random person off the internet. That one layer of trust changes the whole dynamic. Transactions happen faster. People show up. No-shows get flagged.

The vision

We're starting at McMaster because that's where we are. We're building the product right — verification that works, messaging that doesn't leak personal info, ratings that actually filter out bad actors. When we get it right, we expand to the next school. Then the next. Every university and college across Canada is on the list.

How Loot makes money

Free to list, free to browse. Buyers can add Loot Buyer Protection at checkout for an optional 8% fee on the item price — paid by the buyer on top, not deducted from the seller. No fee on cash, e-transfer, or trades. In-app Stripe payments are live in V1.
