Why Loot

Built for campus.
Not for randos.

Generic marketplaces weren't designed for people sharing buildings. Loot is.

01

Verified students only

Sign up with your .ca university email, re-verify once a year. Every Loot user is a real classmate — no graduates, no outsiders.

02

Buy, sell & rent

One app for selling textbooks, renting out your calculator, or swapping a jacket. Free to list — up to 10 active listings on the free plan.

03

Search by course code

Type "ECON 1B03" and see every relevant textbook on campus, sorted by how close the seller is to you.

04

Ratings & reviews

Every meetup ends with a quick rating. Build a rep across your years on campus — flakes get filtered fast.

How it works

Three steps.
No middleman.

Verify with your school email, find what you need on campus, and pay through Loot when you're ready. Every in-app payment includes an 8% transaction fee and buyer protection.

01

Sign up with your university email

Verify with your .ca email when you sign up, then once a year after that. Keeps the marketplace student-only as people graduate.

02

Browse or post a listing

Snap a few photos, set a price (or pick "open to trade"), tag the course code. Done in 30 seconds. Free to list.

03

Meet on campus & pay through Loot

Chat in the app, meet at MUSC or the library to inspect the item, then pay through Loot. Stripe holds your payment until you confirm. Buyer protection is included. Rate each other after.
