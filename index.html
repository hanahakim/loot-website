How it works

Three steps to your next sale.

01

Verify with your .ca email

Sign up with your university or college email. We confirm your enrollment in seconds — no ID upload, no waiting period.

02

List or browse

Post a listing in 30 seconds — photo, price, title. Or browse your campus feed for textbooks, electronics, furniture, and more.

03

Meet on campus, close the deal

Message the seller in the app, agree on a campus meetup spot, and pay however you both want. Loot never touches the money.
