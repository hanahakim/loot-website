Why Loot

Built for campus.
Not for randos.

Generic marketplaces weren't designed for people sharing buildings. Loot is.

01

Verified students only

Sign up with your .ca university email, re-verify once a year. Every Loot user is a real classmate — no graduates, no outsiders.

02

Buy, sell & rent

One app for selling your old textbooks, renting out your calculator, or swapping that jacket. Free to list, free to browse.

How it works

Three steps.
No middleman.

Loot doesn't hold your money or touch your stuff. We just make sure you're meeting another verified student.

01

Sign up with your university email

Verify with your .ca email when you sign up, then once a year after that. Keeps the marketplace student-only as people graduate.

02

Browse or post a listing

Snap a few photos, set a price (or pick "open to trade"), tag the course code. Done in 30 seconds.

03

Meet on campus & close the deal

Chat in the app, meet at MUSC or the library, pay by e-transfer or cash. Rate each other after.
