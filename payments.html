How payments work.

Loot uses Stripe to process payments securely. Here's exactly what happens to your money.

For everyone

Clear for buyers.
Simple for sellers.

For buyers

  • Pay in-app at checkout. Enter your card once. Loot uses Stripe — the same payment infrastructure as Amazon, Shopify, and Uber.
  • Funds are held briefly. Your payment is held securely while you complete the meetup. You have 48 hours to confirm or dispute after meeting the seller.
  • Covered by buyer protection. Every in-app payment is backed by Loot's buyer protection policy. If something goes wrong, we make it right.

For sellers

  • Get paid directly to your bank. Loot uses Stripe Connect to send payouts to your Canadian bank account. One-time setup, under 5 minutes.
  • Service fee. Loot may charge a service fee of up to 5% on completed in-app sales — covering payment processing, buyer protection, and keeping the platform running. You see the exact amount before confirming any listing.
  • Payout timing. Funds are released to your bank within 2–7 business days after the buyer confirms the transaction. Standard Stripe payout schedule.
Security

Your money is safe.

Loot never stores your card details. Payments are handled entirely by Stripe — one of the most trusted payment processors in the world.

PCI DSS compliant

All payments processed by Stripe, which is certified to the highest standard in payment security.

Zero card storage

Loot never stores your card details. They go straight to Stripe's encrypted vault, not our servers.

Bank-level encryption

Every transaction uses TLS encryption end-to-end. Your data moves in a locked box.

Funds frozen during disputes

If a buyer opens a dispute, the seller's payout is frozen immediately until we review and decide.
