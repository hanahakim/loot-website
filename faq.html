Verification
Any .ca university or college email. For McMaster: @mcmaster.ca, @student.mcmaster.ca, and @mail.mcmaster.ca. We expand accepted domains as we launch at new schools. If your school email isn't working, email us and we'll sort it out.
Yes. You re-verify with your university email once a year. This keeps the marketplace clean — accounts belonging to students who've graduated or left can't stay active indefinitely. It takes one click when your annual prompt comes up. In certain cases — a dispute, a flagged account, or a high-value transaction — we may request additional ID verification.
Check your junk/spam folder — university email filters are aggressive. If it's not there after a few minutes, use "Resend verification email" on the verification screen. Still nothing? Email support@lootapp.ca.
Transactions
Pay through Loot via Stripe at checkout. Your payment is held until you confirm the meetup. An 8% transaction fee applies — buyer protection is included automatically. Cash and e-transfer are allowed off-platform with no fee and no protection.
Leave an honest rating and report the user. No-shows are a serious breach of community standards. Patterns of no-shows result in account suspension. Repeat offenders are permanently banned. Verified accounts have real reputations on the line.
Always inspect items before paying. If something isn't right, you're under no obligation to complete the transaction. If you've already paid through Loot and feel misled, open a dispute in the app. Cash and e-transfer deals are not covered.
Sellers mark a listing as "Rent" and set a price per day, week, or month, with available dates. Terms — duration, deposit, condition — are agreed in chat. Return logistics are handled between the two parties. We recommend using the same spot for pickup and return.
