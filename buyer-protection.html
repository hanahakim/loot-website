Built into every transaction

Buyer Protection.

Choose Loot Buyer Protection at checkout — 8% fee, dispute coverage included. If something goes wrong on a protected payment, we make it right.

What's covered

Three situations where
we have your back.

01

Item not as described

You receive something significantly different from the listing — wrong textbook edition, broken electronics listed as working, major undisclosed damage. Open a dispute on a protected payment and the charge is cancelled or refunded.

02

Seller no-show

Seller takes your payment and never shows up. You get a full refund, automatically. No questions asked. The seller is flagged and investigated — repeat no-shows result in a permanent ban.

03

Fraudulent listing

Item doesn't exist or seller doesn't own it. Full refund, plus the seller is permanently banned and reported. Zero tolerance for fraud on a campus platform.

What's not covered

Be honest with yourself, too.

Buyer protection exists for genuine problems — not for changing your mind. Here's what falls outside the policy.

  • Buyer's remorse. You inspected it, agreed to it, bought it. That's a completed transaction.
  • Minor differences from listing photos. Lighting, slight wear, a small scuff not mentioned — these are not disputes. Major damage is.
  • Disputes filed more than 48 hours after the meetup. File promptly or you lose the option. Check the item at the meetup.
  • In-app payments without Buyer Protection. You can pay through Loot without adding protection — but disputes are not available on those transactions.
  • Transactions completed off-platform (cash, e-transfer). Protection only applies when you add Loot Buyer Protection at checkout. Cash deals are buyer's risk.
  • Rental damage caused by the renter. If you broke it, that's on you. The deposit system exists for exactly this reason.
How it works

From checkout to
protected in four steps.

01

Choose Buyer Protection at checkout

Add Loot Buyer Protection when you pay — 8% of the item price, paid by you on top. Dispute coverage is included. Skip it and disputes are not available.

02

Pay through Loot

Stripe authorizes your payment at checkout and holds it until you confirm the meetup. The seller is not paid until you tap confirm.

03

Meet on campus & inspect

Physically check the item before confirming. If it's wrong, don't confirm — open a dispute instead.

04

Confirm or dispute

Happy with it? Confirm and payment releases to the seller. Something wrong? Open a dispute on a protected payment — the charge is cancelled or refunded immediately and the seller payout does not go through.

—

Rental deposits

Damage claims on rentals use a separate process — renters have 24 hours to dispute a lender's damage claim before a security deposit is captured.

—

Questions?

Email support@lootapp.ca with your transaction ID. We respond within one business day.

⚠ Cash and e-transfer deals are not covered.

Buyer protection only applies when you add Loot Buyer Protection at checkout and pay through Stripe in the app. If you pay without protection, or agree to cash or e-transfer outside the app, we can't help if something goes wrong. Stay in the app — that's what the protection is for.
