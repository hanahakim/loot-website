How it works

Search by course code,
not by title.

No more googling ISBNs or hoping the bookstore has your edition. Search the course code and see exactly what students at your school are selling.

01

Type your course code

Enter the exact code — e.g. COMMERCE 1E03. Results show only listings tagged with that code by verified students at your university.

02

Filter and compare

Sort by condition, edition, and price. See what students paid new vs what they're asking now. Make an informed decision before you message anyone.

03

Message the seller directly

All conversations happen in-app. Agree on the meetup spot, confirm the edition, pay through Loot — done. You never need to share your personal number.

Buy or rent

Both options available.
You decide.

Buy it

Own it for the semester, then sell it again on Loot when you're done. Most students recoup 60–80% of what they paid — sometimes more if the course code is still active. It's the closest thing to borrowing without the library wait list.

Rent it

Need it for one semester only? Rent directly from another student. Agree on the duration and terms in the app chat. Security deposit holds via Stripe are supported in V1 — agree on the deposit amount in-app before the meetup.

⚠ Always confirm the edition before buying.

A $60 textbook for the wrong edition is $60 wasted. Check your course outline for the required edition before messaging the seller — and confirm it in the chat before you pay. The edition, ISBN, and any required access codes should be agreed on before the meetup.
