Verified students only

Everyone on Loot is
a real student.

We built the trust layer that generic marketplaces never bothered with. Here's exactly how it works.

01

University email verification

Every Loot account requires a valid .ca university or college email. We send a verification link before you can post or message anyone. Accounts re-verify annually — so when someone graduates or leaves, their access doesn't carry over. In certain cases — disputes, flagged accounts, or high-value transactions — we may request additional government or student ID verification. No workarounds.

02

Ratings after every deal

Both parties rate each other after a transaction. Ratings are public on your profile. Patterns of bad behaviour get accounts reviewed and flagged.

03

Report any listing or user

Every listing and profile has a report button. Reports go to our team. We review within 24 hours and take action — from warning to permanent ban.

04

Private messaging

All communication stays inside the Loot app. You never have to share your phone number or personal email to arrange a transaction.

Smart meetups

Tips for safe, smooth transactions.

1

Meet in public, on campus

The library, the student centre, a campus coffee shop — these are all great spots. Avoid private residences for first-time exchanges with someone you don't know.

2

Check items before paying

Physically inspect items before handing over payment. If a textbook has extra highlighting or a calculator doesn't turn on, that's your moment to renegotiate or walk away.

3

Use the chat to document the deal

Agree on condition, price, and meetup spot in the Loot chat before you meet. That log is available if a dispute ever comes up.

4

Rate honestly

Ratings only work if they're accurate. A quick, honest rating after every transaction keeps the community healthy for everyone.

5

Something went wrong?

Email support@lootapp.ca with your transaction details. We review every dispute personally and respond within one business day.

Questions about safety?

We're a small team and we read every email. Reach out directly — we'll get back to you.
