How it works

Simple as a hallway handoff.

Verify with your school email, find what you need on campus, and pay through Loot when you're ready. Every in-app payment runs through Stripe and includes buyer protection.

01

Sign up with your university email

Use your .ca email — McMaster, U of T, Queen's, wherever. We send a one-click verification link. Re-verify once a year to keep your account active. In certain cases — disputes, flagged accounts, or high-value transactions — we may ask for additional ID verification.

02

Browse or post a listing

Snap a few photos, add a price (or pick "open to trade"), tag the course code for textbooks. Listings go live in under 30 seconds. Free to list, no limits.

03

Meet on campus & pay through Loot

Chat in-app, agree on a spot — MUSC, the library, your residence lobby. Inspect the item, then pay at checkout through Loot. Stripe holds your payment until you confirm — covered by buyer protection. Rate each other after.

The details

—

Verification is permanent

You verify with your university email when you sign up, then once every year after that. One click — keeps the marketplace student-only as people graduate and move on.

—

In-app messaging

All chats happen inside Loot. No need to share your phone number or personal email. Conversations stay tied to the listing.

—

Trade without money

Mark any listing as "open to trade" and describe what you're looking for. Students propose their own items — no awkward negotiating.

—

Ratings after every deal

Both sides rate each other out of 5 after a transaction. Profiles show your average — flakes get filtered fast.
