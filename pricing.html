Payments

How fees work.

When a buyer pays through Loot, a transaction fee applies on the item price — paid by the buyer on top, not deducted from the seller. The seller receives the full listing price. Buyer protection is included on every in-app payment. No add-ons, no tiers at checkout.

Free accounts: 8% per in-app transaction. Loot Pro (coming soon): 4% per in-app transaction. Protection is the same either way.

Cash and e-transfer between students carry no fee and no buyer protection. Trades are always free.
